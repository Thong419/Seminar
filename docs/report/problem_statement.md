# Problem Statement

The core problem is to determine whether a news article or claim is likely fake or real while also providing actionable evidence for the decision. A practical system must satisfy several requirements:

- Produce a reliable label.
- Provide calibrated confidence and trust signals.
- Retrieve evidence that supports or contradicts the prediction.
- Explain the decision in human-readable form.
- Remain robust to noisy or adversarial text.
- Protect privacy, reduce bias, and support responsible use.

The challenge is not only classification accuracy but also trustworthiness, interpretability, and operational readiness.
